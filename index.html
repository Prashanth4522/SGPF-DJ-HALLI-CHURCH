Our Story

About our church

Established in 1991, ours is a story of faith, vision, and miracles. From a small garage meeting to a thriving community of over 700 believers.

Our history

Established in 1991. In the beginning it was a garage—an Ambassador car, a fridge, and a small cell meeting. The owner of the property, Uncle Edward, had received a vision from God: this place would become a house of worship where many would gather. At the same time, in another location, God spoke to Pastor Deena Dayalan to start the church right here. Edward's vision became mission.

Uncle Edward resigned from his job. The cell meeting turned into a church, with just thirty members. As the years passed, God brought in many more—delivering people from bondages, demonic oppression, and poverty. Thirty grew to sixty, sixty to a hundred and twenty. One service became two, then three. Today we are over seven hundred believers. All glory belongs to God alone.

Our youth ministry grew too: about eighty young people were formed into three teams, with lively scripture competitions and a heart to serve. We are grateful for every life the Lord has added to this family.

All glory belongs to God alone—and to none else.

Our vision

To love God, love people, and build lives through the Word, prayer, and fellowship.

What to expect

Warm welcome, worship, Scripture-based message, and prayer. Families and first-time visitors are always welcome.

For families
